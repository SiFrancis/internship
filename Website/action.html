TOP ACTION GAMES


ELDEN RING

Fusing the worlds of George R.R. Martin and Hidetaka Miyazaki, the minds behind Game of Thrones and Dark Souls, comes Elden Ring: the new frontier of open-world gaming. Restore order to The Lands Between, as a Tarnished soul who's destined to restore the Great Elden Ring and bring balance back to the world. Fulfil your destiny.



GOD OF WAR RAGNAROK

The sequel to the 2018 Sony Santa Monica title, God of War Ragnarok is the next chapter in the story of Kratos and his growing son Atreus as they prepare for the titular cataclysm that threatens to be unleashed upon not just the Norse lands of Midgard, but all of the nine realms.


HORIZON FORBIDDEN WEST

Horizon Forbidden West continues the story of Aloy half a year after the events of the Zero Dawn, a young huntress of the Nora tribe sent on a quest to a mysterious frontier spanning Utah to the Pacific coast to find the source of a mysterious plague that kills all it infects. On her journey across the uncharted lands of the Forbidden West, she encounters hostile regions filled with natural threats and ravaged by massive storms, dangerous enemies and deadly machines, both new and old.


DYING LIGHT 2: STAY HUMAN

After a devastating disease wipes out billions of humans leaving only hordes of flesh-eating monsters and a handful of survivors, you must make decisions and fight to survive. You choose who to side with, you choose who lives and dies, but most importantly, you choose your own fate. In a world filled with hundreds of decisions and hordes of flesh-eating zombies, you must find a way to survive against man and monster. Also, this happens only in the daytime; at night, things tend to get just a tiny bit more freakish.


BAYONETTA 3

In Bayonetta 3, the story moves beyond the fight between Paradiso and Inferno, as reality is put danger. An entity known as Singularity unleashes the Homunculi throughout the multiverse in an attempt to destroy each world without it. In order to stop this, Bayonetta must travel across these fractured realms in order to recover the Chaos Gears, meeting other Bayonettas from various timelines along the way.
