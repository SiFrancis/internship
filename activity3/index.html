FAQ

Who owns Coffee Project?

Coffee Project is a chain of coffee shops under the AllValue Corp , the retail arm of the Villar Group of Companies. The man behind the vision of Coffee Project is none other than Manny B. Villar.

What are your store hours?

Coffee Project's regular store hours are from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM though store hours may vary per store such as branches outside Metro Manila.

Do you deliver?

Coffee Project accepts deliveries through the following channels:

  • Coffee Project Marketplace - https://shop.coffeeproject.com.ph
  • SMS - 09666290203 / 09666336662 / 09175353315
  • Delivery partners - GetAll, FoodPanda, GrabFood, Mangan, Pandalivery, Lalamove, Pickaroo, Ride Eat, 188 Express, Joyride, HappyMove, Papa PH, Food Trip, KartHero, Gapan Grabb, and Pabili Express Gapan.
As of the moment, Coffee Project doesn't accept late night deliveries because we do not have any branches yet that's open for 24 hours.
